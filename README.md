# 📊 Global Sales Analysis

Analysis of global sales data across 12 product categories, 47 countries, and 8 years (2010–2017), including online and offline channels.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- **Google Sheets** — data cleaning, pivot tables, charts

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Orders | 1,331 |
| Total Revenue | $1,702,129,408 |
| Total Profit | $501,434,459 |
| Profit Margin | ~29.5% |
| Countries Covered | 47 |

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Cosmetics** has the highest profit ($92.7M) despite not being the top revenue category — superior margins.
- **Office Supplies** leads in revenue ($402.2M).
- **Europe** accounts for 88.5% of total revenue; Asia contributes only 5.5%.
- Online vs Offline split is nearly equal: **51.2% Offline / 48.8% Online**.
